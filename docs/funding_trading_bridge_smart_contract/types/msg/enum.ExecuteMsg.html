All defined paylods to be used when executing routes on this contract instance.

Variants§

§

AdminUpdateAdmin

Fields

§new_admin_address: String

A bech32 address to use as the new administrator of the contract.

A route that swaps the current value in the contract state for the admin to the provided value.

§

AdminUpdateDepositRequiredAttributes

Fields

§attributes: Vec<String>

The new attributes that will be set in the contract state’s required_deposit_attributes property upon successful execution.

A route that sets a new collection of attribute names required when an account deposits their deposit denom into the contract via the fund_trading execution route.

§

AdminUpdateWithdrawRequiredAttributes

Fields

§attributes: Vec<String>

The new attributes that will be set in the contract state’s required_withdraw_attributes property upon successful execution.

A route that sets a new collection of attribute names required when an account withdraws their deposit denom from the contract via the withdraw_trading execution route.

§

FundTrading

Fields

§trade_amount: Uint128

The amount of the deposit marker to pull from the sender’s account in exchange for trading denom.

A route that will attempt to pull the trade amount of the deposit marker’s denom from the sender’s account with a marker transfer, discern how much of the trading denom to which the submitted amount is equivalent, and then mint and withdraw the equivalent amount into the sender’s account.

§

WithdrawTrading

Fields

§trade_amount: Uint128

The amount of the trading marker to pull from the sender’s account in exchange for deposit denom.

A route that will attempt to pull the trade amount of the trading marker’s denom from the sender’s account with a marker transfer, discern how much of the deposit denom to which the submitted amount is equivalent, transfer that amount to the sender, and then burn the exchanged trading marker denom.
